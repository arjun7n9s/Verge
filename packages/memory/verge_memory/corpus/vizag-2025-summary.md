# Vizag 2025 Public-Incident Summary Stub

In January 2025, public reports described a fatal industrial gas incident at
Visakhapatnam Steel Plant. Verge treats this as a reconstructed demo scenario,
not ground truth: gas readings, permit activity, shift-change timing, and source
lineage are replayed synthetically so the eval harness can prove behavior
against baselines without claiming measured plant data.

Relevant pattern: rising flammable or toxic gas, hot-work or confined-space
activity, and shift handover can combine into a higher-risk state before any
single threshold trips.
